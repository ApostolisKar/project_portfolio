Project 4

November 26, 2024

In today’s competitive market, customer segmentation is a cornerstone of successful marketing. It provides the ability to:

  • Understand customer demographics and behaviors, enabling the creation of effective strategies.
  • Spot spending patterns and engagement trends, allowing for greater personalization.
  • Develop tailored campaigns, improving both impact and conversion rates.

This project focused on identifying distinct customer groups based on their unique characteristics and behaviors. By doing so, the marketing team could design highly targeted and impactful campaigns tailored to each segment, based on data-driven insights.

Steps followed:

  • Investigated relationships between customers and their purchasing habits.

  • Tested over 10 algorithms.

  • Key Features: Income, age, marital status, number of children, education, product engagement, campaign participation.

  • K-means algorithm was chosen for simplicity, scalability, and performance.

  • 3 Clusters identified:

  1. Low income, families with small children
  2. Medium income, families with teenagers
  3. No kids, high spenders
